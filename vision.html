My Vision

developing bubbles :)

my long-term vision is to harness the power of artificial intelligence and innovative slum mapping techniques to create meaningful pathways between dedicated educators and underserved students in marginalized communities. by identifying educational gaps and connecting resources to those in need, i hope to help transform individual lives and uplift entire communities. my ambition extends beyond education — i aspire to become a ceo who leads with purpose, building organizations that drive impact across multiple industries. through a commitment to equity, innovation, and social responsibility, i aim to leave a lasting legacy that empowers future generations.
