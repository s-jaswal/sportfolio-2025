i have been actively involved in the web design program for three years, gaining hands-on experience in front-end development, user interface design, and responsive layouts. as a member of the program's advisory board, i help guide curriculum decisions and mentor newer students. my project work has consistently centered around stem-related themes, allowing me to explore the intersection of technology, design, and real-world problem solving. through these experiences, i've developed a strong foundation in creating meaningful digital solutions that reflect both functionality and innovation.
